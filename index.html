About Us

Central NJ Infosec is a loose group of security professionals in the Central NJ area. We have monthly meetings that do have rules.

Meetings

We meet monthly on the last Wednesday of the month. Meetings include the following format:

  • Short "Turbo Talks" from anyone who would like to speak.
  • Talks should be between 5-10 min in length.
  • Topics can include anything relevent to hacking/infosec.
  • Longer talks may be accepted as long as submitted prior to meetings.
  • If this is your first time, come prepared to speak.

    Participation

    Participation is mandatory for all involved with Central NJ Infosec. We all want to know what you are working on and the best way is for you to speak about it.

    Presentation Ideas

    Often figuring out what to present on can be the most challenging. Below are some ideas to help you:

  • Present your own material/work
  • We love code, demo’s and live examples of work. But make it your own. Don’t give us a 101 on a topic that any of us could just lookup ourselves or read an already written how-to on.
  • It doesn’t have to be finished worked
  • If you’ve shown you’re doing your own research but are stuck, come talk to us and we’ll likely be able to help!
  • No book reports
  • Don’t tell us all about someone elses research that you read and now are regurgitating to us. Expand on it! Add to it!

    Out of Town?

    From out of town but want to join a meeting? Great! We would love to have you. Presentation is not required for you, but we would still love to hear what you are up to.
